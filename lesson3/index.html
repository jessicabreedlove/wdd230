Site Purpose

The purpose of pgcommerce.org is to promote local businesses in the Pleasant Grove, Utah area. Consider this site a resource if you want to help local business or if you are a business owner in the city of Pleasant Grove. We know the power of a unified community and consider it our mission to bring our citizens together.
