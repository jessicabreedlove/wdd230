Pleasant Grove Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Target Audience

  • Residents, future residents, or visitors of Pleasant Grove, UT
  • Ages 21 and older
  • Have an income between $46,000 - $90,000 per year
  • Are interested in shopping locally, have or are starting a local business, or want to support the community

Personas

  1. Jane Doe

    2. Jane is a aspiring entrapenuer. She is 33 yrs old and passionate about handcrafted goods. She lives in Pleasant Grove with her family. She is excited to visit the PG Chamber of Commerce site and use the resourses available there to get her local business start up off the ground.

  2. John Doe

    3. John has lived in Pleasant Grove his whole life. He has a small bakery in town that he has had for the past 10 years. He is 50 yrs old and loves to visit the PG Chamber of Commerce site to access the tools there to help grow and sustain his local business.
