Principles of Design

White Space and Clean Design

Real Heroines

http://www.realheroines.com/

The Real Heroines websites does an excellent job using white space! Each person is simply a portrait with a name and title, with plenty of room around each person.

Alignment

YouTube

https://www.youtube.com/

YouTube is a master of alignment! The site uses many rows and columns, adjusted to your viewport, to show numerous collections of content.

Contrast

PluralSight

https://www.pluralsight.com/

PluralSight uses a strong example of contrast on their site. With a black background, white text and minimal highlights of strong color, options are highlightes.
